Format & Scoring

Match Format: Singles draws will have 32 players; all singles draws will have a Feed-in Consolation (FIC) that feeds players in through the Round of 16. The Quarterfinal losers will be fed into a separate “Quarter-Consolation” draw to a playoff for 5th and 6th place. The FIC shall be played to completion.
Tiebreaks: Singles matches will be 2 out of 3 sets, regular scoring, with a 10-point tiebreaker in lieu of a 3rd set. Doubles matches will be 8-game prosets, no-ad scoring, with a tiebreaker at 8-games all.
