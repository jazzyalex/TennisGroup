Welcome to Summer Tennis Mini Group! Designed for U10/U12 tournament players with a UTR of 1.5-3 (flexible) our program focus on developing consistency and mindful play through competitive games that push kids to level up their skills. Our approach ensures that every player gains valuable experience and improves their game significantly.

What to Expect

Shot Tolerance Training: Using both yellow and green balls, we work on extending rallies and improving your shot tolerance—a crucial skill at any level of play.

Defensive Skills Development: Learn how to turn defense into offense with drills that enhance your ability to stay in the point.

Live Game Experience: Experience real match situations with single and double matches, helping you apply what you've learned in a competitive setting.

Serve Drills and Games: Master your serve with targeted drills and strategic games designed to improve accuracy and consistency.

Movement Challenges: Improve your footwork and agility with dynamic movement challenges, inspired by the Spanish method, ensuring focused and intensive training.

Small Group, Big Impact

With only up to 4 kids per court, our sessions ensure personalized attention and ample opportunity for each player to practice and improve.

Consistent Training for Consistent Results

"To be solid brings confidence and reduces anxiety. To be solid does not mean to be a defensive player." – Sergi Bruguera

Consistent players win a lot of tennis matches, and consistency is the foundation for future high-level play. Our training program is built on this principle, helping young players develop the resilience and skills needed for long-term success.

Led by a PTR Affiliate Rec Coach

Our sessions are led by a PTR Rec Coach affiliate, ensuring top-quality instruction and adherence to the highest coaching standards.

Who Can Join?

U10 and U12 tennis tournament players with a UTR of 1.5-3 (flexible) looking to elevate their game and gain valuable match experience.
